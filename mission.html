Our Mission

What We Do

Neighborhood Voice Alliance Inc. supports local area groups who are working to preserve and protect their communities. We believe in protecting the historic character of communities for current residents and future generations.

Core Objectives

  • • Fund and support community groups fighting against overdevelopment and zoning violations
  • • Protect communities from spot zoning that threatens neighborhood character
  • • Support community youth in their personal, civic, and educational development
  • • Preserve essential community character for current and future generations
