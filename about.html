About Us

Charlestown Marina - Where Historic Charm Meets Modern Boston

Who We Are

Neighborhood Voice Alliance Inc. is a Massachusetts Not for Profit Corporation and a tax deductible 501(c)3 organization dedicated to preserving the character and quality of life in our communities.

Our Purpose

We support local communities in their efforts to maintain neighborhood character, preserve historic landmarks, and ensure responsible development that benefits all residents.

Current Focus

We are actively supporting the Charlestown community in their legal efforts to ensure responsible development that aligns with community needs and preserves neighborhood character.
